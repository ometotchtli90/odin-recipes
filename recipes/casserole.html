Cassarole Recipe

Why is this the best casserole?

Perfect dinner which can be combined with alot of leftover Ingredients you may have.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Broccoli
  • Sweet Potato
  • Vegan Butter
  • Onion
  • Flour
  • Plant-based Heavy Cream
  • Yeast
  • Nutmeg
  • Dry Rosemary
  • Dry Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes, sweet potatoes, onion. wash and cut broccoli
  2. Melt vegan butter in a pot and fry onion for 3 minutes - dust with flour and heat for 2 minutes
  3. Stir cream in, season with yeast, salt, pepper, nutmeg, dry rosemary, dry thyme
  4. preheat oven to 180Â°C
  5. grease baking dish with vegan butter
  6. add potatoes, sweet potatoes, broccoli and pour the cream over
  7. mix cheese, oil, water and put it on top
  8. bake for 40 minutes
