Lasagna Recipe

Why is this the best lasagna?

This vegan lasagna recipe with white sauce, tomato sauce, green lentils, and dairy-free cheese is an absolute highlight!

Ingredients

  • French Lentils
  • Carrots
  • Fresh Basil
  • Unsweetened Soy Milk
  • Red Wine
  • Chili
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leat
  • Tomatoes
  • Vegan Cheese
  • Bechamel Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot with olive oil, add onion, garlic, carrot, chili pepper, bay leaves - saute for 5 minutes
  2. add lentils and red wine - cook for 3 minutes
  3. mash tomatoes and stir them in water and fresh basil - simmer for 30 minutes, season with salt and pepper
  4. melt the dairy-free butter in a pot, add flour - stir for 1 minute, then pour soy milk in - then simmer for 10 minutes, season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
  5. proheat oven to 200Â°C
  6. spread thin layer of bechamel, followed by 3 sheets lasagna noodles, spread 1/3 of lentil bolognese on top, followed by layer of bechamel, and 1/3 ov vegan cheese
  7. repeat two times
  8. bake for 30 minutes - sit for 10 minutes
